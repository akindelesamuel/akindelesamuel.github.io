THE UNTOLD STORY

The purpose of this website is to provide interesting and useful facts about Africa. Information on such topics as the people of Africa, Africa landforms, African art history, African animals, African jewelry, and African clothing will be covered. Africa is the most interesting and diverse continent on the earth. It is the home of an incredible variety of people, animals, climates, and spectacular places. It is a country known for its beautiful colorful clothing and its history of incredible works of art. Africa is where human beings first appeared on earth and the home of the world's first great civilization, Egypt which dates back to 3300 BC. Below you will find a few lists of some interesting facts about Africa

PLACES

MOUNT KILIMANJARO

Rising to 19,340 ft above the plains of Africa, Kili (as Mount Kilimanjaro is popularly known) is the highest mountain in Africa. It is definitely one of the highlights of any trip to Tanzania.

VICTORIA FALLS

The grandiose Victoria Falls are located on the Zambezi River. These waterfalls are the largest moving body of water on Earth and you have to be there to see them for yourself!

THE ZULU

The Zulu tribe are one of the earliest settlers in Africa. They can be found in the Southern part of Africa. Though civilisation has taken over the world, the Zulus are one of the few tribes who have preserve their ancestral history and heriatge.
